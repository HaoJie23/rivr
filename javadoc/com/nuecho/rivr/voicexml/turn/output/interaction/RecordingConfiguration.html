A RecordingConfiguration represents the description of a recording final phase of an interaction.

It may have an optional DtmfRecognitionConfiguration to recognize DTMF input while recording. A DTMF input matching one of the DtmfRecognitionConfiguration grammar will terminate the recording and place the recording in a variable.

See Also:
DtmfRecognitionConfiguration, http://www.w3.org/TR/voicexml20/#dml2.3.6
