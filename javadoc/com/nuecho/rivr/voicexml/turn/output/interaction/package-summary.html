InteractionBuilder Builder used to ease creation of instances of InteractionTurn
InteractionPrompt An InteractionPrompt represent a phase in an InteractionTurn and is composed of a sequence of AudioItem and optionally a speech and/or a dtmf recognition configuration.
InteractionRecognition An InteractionRecognition is an optional final phase of an InteractionTurn.
InteractionRecording An InteractionRecording is an optional final phase of an InteractionTurn.
InteractionTurn An InteractionTurn is a VoiceXmlOutputTurn that represents a list of InteractionPrompt with an optional final recognition or recording phase.
RecordingConfiguration A RecordingConfiguration represents the description of a recording final phase of an interaction.
SpeechRecognitionConfiguration A SpeechRecognitionConfiguration represents the speech part of an interaction phase recognition configuration.
