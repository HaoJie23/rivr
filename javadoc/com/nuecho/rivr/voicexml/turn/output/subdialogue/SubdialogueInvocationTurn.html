public class SubdialogueInvocationTurn
extends VoiceXmlOutputTurn

A SubdialogueInvocationTurn is a VoiceXmlOutputTurn that invokes another external dialogue.

Parameters may be passed to the subdialogue and return values may be retrieved if the invoked subdialogue ends with a <return> instruction.

See Also:
SubdialogueParameter, SubdialogueSubmitMethod, http://www.w3.org/TR/voicexml20/#dml2.3.4
